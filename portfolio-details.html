Company Details

Elias Gulilat Tsige Fruits and Vegetables Exporter is an independent fruits and vegetables exporter company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We carter to a diverse set of clients from- Netherlands, UK, UAE, Djibouti, Somalia, Belgium and Sudan.

About Us

Our dedicated team of experts work closely with the fruits and vegetables farmers in Ethiopia constantly re-innovating the styles of fruits and vegetables production to increase quality and efficiency.

Multi-business owner

At EG Fruits, our journey is rooted in a passion for bringing nature's finest to the world. Established with a vision to share the vibrant flavors and nourishment of fresh fruits and vegetables, we've rapidly grown into a trusted name in the industry. With our headquarters nestled in the heart of fertile farming regions, we have direct access to the most abundant sources of produce. This proximity allows us to meticulously select and deliver the freshest fruits and vegetables to homes, businesses, and markets across the globe.

Our mission extends beyond mere exports; it's a commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We take pride in adhering to the highest industry standards, and our dedication to ethical and sustainable farming practices sets us apart. With every shipment, we aim to bring joy to households and kitchens while preserving the environment. As we continue to expand our reach and explore new horizons, we remain dedicated to our core values of freshness, excellence, and environmental responsibility. At EG Fruits, we look forward to a fruitful future as we share the beauty and goodness of nature with you.
